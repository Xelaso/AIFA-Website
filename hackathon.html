What is AIFAHacks?

AIFA is going to be hosting a free mini-hackathon at the University of Texas at Dallas on September 30th, 2023 to create some sort of application(website, android/ios app, desktop app) using ChatGPT or Bard’s API to promote social good. There will also be a beginner division, in which students with no prior coding experience can use Google Appsheets to develop an application using their AI. During the event, we will equip beginner and advanced students with workshops, such as how to use Bard's API to develop an application(Advanced) and how to use Google Appsheets to use AI to develop an app (Beginner) for the chance to win prizes such as scholarships, cash, and more. We will also provide snacks, drinks, and lunch for students.

Beginners- You will be using Google Appsheets to create an app using AI!
Advanced- You will be using Bard's API to create an app for social good!

Frequently asked questions

  • I have NO EXPERIENCE in coding. Should I still participate?

    DEFINITELY! We have a beginner division and will host workshops in which you don't need to know how to code at all to make an app!

  • Who can participate?

    ANYONE! As long as you are a high school student, you are welcome to join! Again, you don't need any experience

  • How much does it cost?

    It's completely free! Students will get free food, snacks, swag, and more!

  • How many people per group?

    There will be 2-4 students per team!

  • What should I bring?

    Bring a laptop, charger, and anything else you think you would need. We may be able to loan out computers if needed, but we will need to know in advance.

  • Where is this event?

    Engineering and Computer Science Building, 800 W Campbell Rd, Richardson, TX 75080

  • Other Questions?

    Email us at aiforallfoundation@gmail.com!
